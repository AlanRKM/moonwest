About Us

Moonwest Express Inc. is a family-owned company with decades of experience in cargo transportation. We take pride in our dedicated team of skilled drivers and dispatchers, who are committed to delivering every load with the highest standards of care, precision, and on-time performance. Our team understands the importance of each shipment and strives to meet and exceed customer expectations on every mile.

Our company values professionalism, reliability, and the relationships we build with our clients. We have a deep understanding of the logistics industry and are passionate about moving your cargo efficiently and safely across the country. With Moonwest Express Inc., you gain a partner that goes the extra mile to ensure your freight reaches its destination intact, on time, and backed by the strength of our experienced crew.
